NAME:     str - human readable
DATE:     dat - acquisition date of the slide
STAINING: str - staining of the slide
FILENAME: str - filename in repo
ZOOM:     int - approximation of the zoom factor
WIDTH:    flt - width of slide in mm
HEIGHT:   flt - height of slide in mm
XPIXEL:   int - number of pixels in width
YPIXEL:   int - number of pixels in height
XRESOLUTION:  flt - pixels/mm
YRESOLUTION:  flt - pixels/mm
SLIDESCANNER: str - slidescanner used to digitize the slide

date format is YYYY:MM:DD HH:MM:SS

Stainings:
HE: Hematoxylin and eosin stain
